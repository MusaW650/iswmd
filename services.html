Prayers

ISWMD offers the 5 daily prayers lead by qualified imaams.

Timings for the daily prayers are provided on the home page. Jummah prayers are also offered every Friday, check the home page for Jummah timings.


Evening Programs

ISWMD also offers evening halaqas with local scholars, and potlucks before or afterwards.

Keep an eye on the events tab to see the timings for these programs.


Outdoor Facilities

A basketball court and soccer field are available for all to use on the masjid property.
